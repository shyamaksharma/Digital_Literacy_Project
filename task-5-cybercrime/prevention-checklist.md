# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share OTP or PIN with anyone
- Do not click on unknown payment links
- Always verify UPI requests before accepting
- Avoid scanning QR codes from unknown sources
- Use strong passwords for banking apps
- Enable app lock or biometric security
- Regularly check bank transaction history
- Do not trust calls asking for money or details
- For fraud reporting: cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930
