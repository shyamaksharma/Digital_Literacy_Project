# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

## ✅ Do’s
- Keep your profiles professional and also clean and un=clustered 
- Think before posting anything publicly that may or may not harm you
- Use strong privacy settings and ensure accounts are protected
- Share useful and positive content
- Respect others online

## ❌ Don’ts
- Don’t share personal or sensitive information
- Don’t post offensive or inappropriate content
- Don’t engage in online arguments and wasting your time
- Don’t accept random friend requests from people you don't know
- Don’t spread misinformation or hate
